Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned and the onion is softened. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, sugar, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well and bring to a simmer. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  4. Spread a thin layer of the sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Place 4 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, slightly overlapping them.
  5. Spoon about a third of the sauce over the noodles, followed by a third of the mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and ricotta cheese. Repeat this layering two more times, ending with a layer of sauce and cheese on top.
  6. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  7. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  9. Slice and serve the lasagna, and enjoy!
