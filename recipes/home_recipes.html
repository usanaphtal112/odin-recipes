Rwandan Irish Potatoes Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 large Irish potatoes
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water

Instructions:

  1. Peel the Irish potatoes and cut them into small cubes.
  2. Rinse the potato cubes under cold water to remove excess starch.
  3. In a large pot, add water and bring it to a boil. Add the potato cubes and cook for about 10-15 minutes or until they are tender.
  4. Drain the cooked potatoes and set them aside.
  5. In a separate pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  6. Add the chopped onions and minced garlic to the pan. Sauté until the onions are translucent.
  7. Add the diced tomatoes and green bell pepper to the pan. Stir and cook for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened.
  8. Add the cooked potato cubes to the pan and mix them with the sautéed vegetables.
  9. Season with paprika, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Stir well to coat the potatoes and vegetables with the spices.
  10. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let the potatoes simmer for about 5-10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  11. Remove from heat and serve the Rwandan Irish Potatoes while hot.
